NewYork Savagers

NewYork Savagers is the mascot of the Newyork State University for Football. It is designed by Sravani. The mascot Thunders is an alligator which is in green and yellow color.

Fun facts about NewYork Savagers

  • They zigzag to escape predators
  • May pretend to bury a nut to throw off potential thieves.
