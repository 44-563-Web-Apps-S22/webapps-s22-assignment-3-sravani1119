NewYork Savagers

NewYork Savagers is the mascot of the Newyork State University for Football. It is designed by Sravani. The NewYork Thunders is an savagers which is in maroon and plum color.

Fun facts about NewYork Savagers

  • They zigzag to escape predators
  • May pretend to bury a nut to throw off potential thieves.
  • Savagers continue to grow throughout their lifetimes.
